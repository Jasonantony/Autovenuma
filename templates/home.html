Our Story

Naan Jason, oru auto driver oda paiyan. Drivers daily face panra kashtam paathu, “namma fare-la nadakkura namma own app venum” nu decide pannen. Romba naal try pannittu, finally Autovenuma born aayiduchu — Salem-la oru click panninaa auto ready… driver-kum fair, passenger-kum care!

How it works..!

1️⃣ App open pannitu booking pannunga

App open pannitu pickup & drop select pannunga. Amount check pannitu payment pannina, booking instant-ah confirm aagidum. Simple-ah, tension illa.

2️⃣ Driver anna gate-ku varuvanga

Booking aana udane driver anna accept pannuvanga and straight-ah unga gate-ku varuvanga. Auto-la yerumbodhu OTP sollunga — safety-ku adhu dhaan enough.

3️⃣ Safe-ah reach pannuvanga, rating podunga

Destination safe-ah reach pannuvanga. Ride nalla irundhaal driver anna-ku rating podunga — unga one click-u avanga day-e super-aa maathidum!
